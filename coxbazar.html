Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh

Cox's Bazar, known for having the longest natural sea beach in the world, is a paradise for beach lovers. With its golden sands, clear blue waters, and vibrant local culture, it offers a perfect getaway for relaxation and adventure. Whether you're looking to unwind or explore, Cox's Bazar has something for everyone.

Why Visit Cox's Bazar?

  • Longest natural sea beach in the world.
  • Stunning sunrises and sunsets over the Bay of Bengal.
  • Rich cultural heritage and local cuisine.
  • Perfect for water sports and beach activities.

Top Attractions in Cox's Bazar

Himchari National Park

A scenic park with waterfalls, hiking trails, and panoramic views of the Bay of Bengal.

Inani Beach

Known for its crystal-clear waters and coral stones, Inani Beach is a hidden gem.

Ramu Buddhist Village

Explore the ancient Buddhist temples and experience the local culture.

Travel Tips for Cox's Bazar

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Cox's Bazar is from November to March when the weather is pleasant and dry.

Local Customs

Respect the local culture and dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Safety Tips

Be cautious while swimming in the sea and follow lifeguard instructions. Avoid venturing too far into the water.

Sample Itineraries

3-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Cox's Bazar, relax on the beach, and enjoy the sunset.
  • Day 2: Visit Himchari National Park and Inani Beach.
  • Day 3: Explore Ramu Buddhist Village and return with unforgettable memories.

7-Day Itinerary

  • Days 1-3: Follow the 3-day itinerary.
  • Days 4-5: Take a boat trip to St. Martin's Island and explore its coral reefs.
  • Days 6-7: Relax on the beach and enjoy local seafood before heading back.
